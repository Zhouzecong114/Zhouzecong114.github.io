About Me

I was born in China in 2004 and have been passionate about art and design since childhood. I graduated from the Sichuan Fine Arts Institute, one of China's leading art academies, majoring in Craft Art and Jewellery Design. My undergraduate studies allowed me to explore the relationship between material language, craftsmanship, and design thinking — an experience that shaped my understanding of beauty, value, and innovation in fashion.

During my time at the academy, I developed a strong interest in the intersection of design and business, especially how creative ideas can transform into meaningful brand strategies. This passion has inspired me to pursue further study in Fashion Management, where I hope to connect artistic creativity with commercial insight.

I also have a strong sense of social responsibility — I regularly help stray cats and dogs, and I participate in charity and environmental initiatives. These experiences have strengthened my empathy, awareness, and commitment to sustainability.

I look forward to continuing my studies in the United Kingdom, where I can expand my global perspective, refine my professional vision, and contribute my creativity and insight to the evolving fashion industry.
